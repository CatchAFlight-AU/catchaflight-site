Flying Singapore Airlines to Europe: Top Destinations and Routes from Australia


For Australian travelers looking to explore Europe, Singapore Airlines offers a seamless and luxurious flying experience, connecting major Australian cities to key European destinations. Known for its exceptional service, extensive route network, and commitment to comfort, Singapore Airlines provides one of the best ways to travel from Australia to Europe.


Whether you’re flying for business or leisure, Singapore Airlines makes it easy to reach Europe with smooth connections through its hub in Singapore Changi Airport, one of the world’s top-rated airports. Let’s explore some of the top destinations in Europe and the best routes Singapore Airlines offers from Australia.


1. London, United Kingdom

London is one of the most sought-after European destinations for both tourists and business travelers. With Singapore Airlines, travelers from Australia can enjoy a smooth connection from cities like Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, and Brisbane to London Heathrow (LHR). The airline operates several daily flights, with a comfortable layover in Singapore that allows you to recharge at Changi Airport before continuing on to the UK’s bustling capital city.


Singapore Airlines’ Business Class and Premium Economy options provide extra comfort on this long-haul route, ensuring that you arrive in London feeling refreshed and ready to explore iconic landmarks like Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the Tower of London.


2. Paris, France

Paris, the City of Light, is another popular destination for Australian travelers. Singapore Airlines offers convenient connections from Australia to Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG), allowing you to experience the charm and beauty of Paris with ease. Whether you’re visiting for leisure to see landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum, or traveling for business, Singapore Airlines ensures a comfortable journey.


For those looking to experience true luxury, the First Class Suites on Singapore Airlines’ Airbus A380 provide an unparalleled experience, with private suites and world-class dining that make the long journey to Paris a pleasure.


3. Frankfurt, Germany

Frankfurt is a major financial hub and a gateway to central Europe. Singapore Airlines operates regular flights from Australia to Frankfurt Airport (FRA), making it an ideal choice for business travelers heading to Germany or other parts of Europe. From Frankfurt, you can easily connect to cities like Munich, Berlin, and Zurich, making Singapore Airlines a convenient option for multi-destination trips.


Business travelers can take advantage of Singapore Airlines’ KrisFlyer Gold Lounge at Changi Airport, as well as the excellent in-flight amenities that allow you to work or relax during the flight.


4. Amsterdam, Netherlands

Amsterdam, with its picturesque canals and rich cultural heritage, is another must-visit European destination. Singapore Airlines offers flights to Amsterdam Schiphol Airport (AMS), connecting Australian travelers with one of Europe’s most vibrant cities. From iconic sites like the Van Gogh Museum to the stunning architecture of Anne Frank’s House, Amsterdam offers something for everyone.


Singapore Airlines’ Premium Economy class on long-haul flights is an excellent choice for travelers who want extra space and comfort without the price tag of Business Class, making the journey to Amsterdam both enjoyable and affordable.


5. Zurich, Switzerland

For travelers seeking both business opportunities and natural beauty, Zurich is a top European destination. Singapore Airlines flies directly to Zurich Airport (ZRH) from Australia, with a stopover in Singapore. Zurich’s position as a global financial center and gateway to Switzerland’s breathtaking Alps makes it a key destination for both work and play.


The Book the Cook service, available in Business and First Class, allows travelers to pre-order gourmet meals and enjoy fine dining during their flight, adding a special touch to your journey to Zurich.


6. Rome, Italy

Italy’s capital city, Rome, is a destination filled with history, culture, and world-class cuisine. With Singapore Airlines, you can fly from Australia to Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO) via Singapore, enjoying a smooth and luxurious journey along the way. From the Colosseum to the Vatican City, Rome is a city that offers endless exploration.


Travelers can take advantage of Singapore Airlines’ extensive KrisFlyer rewards program, earning miles on their journey to redeem for upgrades, additional services, or future flights.


7. Barcelona, Spain

For those looking to explore the Mediterranean coast, Barcelona is a perfect destination. With connections from Australia to Barcelona El Prat Airport (BCN) via Singapore, travelers can explore the vibrant Catalonian culture, visit landmarks like the Sagrada Família, and relax on the city’s beautiful beaches.


The KrisWorld entertainment system on Singapore Airlines’ A380 and 777 aircraft ensures that you are entertained throughout your flight, with a wide range of movies, TV shows, and music at your fingertips.
