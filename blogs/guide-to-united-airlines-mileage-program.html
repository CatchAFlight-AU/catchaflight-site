A Guide to United Airlines MileagePlus Program for Australian Travelers


For Australian travelers, the United Airlines MileagePlus Program offers a great way to earn and redeem points on flights, hotel stays, and more. Whether you’re a frequent flyer or just starting out, MileagePlus provides numerous benefits that can make your travels more rewarding.

In this guide, we’ll explore how Australian travelers can take full advantage of the United Airlines MileagePlus Program, from earning miles to enjoying elite status perks and redeeming rewards.


How to Earn Miles with United MileagePlus

Earning miles with United Airlines MileagePlus is easy, and there are multiple ways to accumulate points, not just by flying. For Australian travelers, here’s how you can start earning:

  • Flights: Earn miles for every flight you take with United Airlines, as well as with partner airlines in the Star Alliance network.
  • Hotel Stays: Earn additional miles by booking stays at partner hotels through the MileagePlus program.
  • Car Rentals: Renting a car with one of United’s car rental partners can also help you rack up more points.
  • Credit Card Purchases: Although specific MileagePlus credit cards aren’t available in Australia, you can still earn points by using general rewards cards that allow you to transfer points to MileagePlus.

Earning Miles on Star Alliance Partner Airlines

As part of the Star Alliance, United Airlines allows MileagePlus members to earn miles on partner airlines, including Singapore Airlines, Air New Zealand, and Thai Airways. This gives Australian travelers even more opportunities to earn miles when flying on international routes.


How to Redeem MileagePlus Miles

Once you’ve accumulated miles, there are plenty of ways to redeem them. Here’s how Australian travelers can make the most of their MileagePlus rewards:

  • Free Flights: Redeem your miles for free or discounted flights with United Airlines or partner airlines in the Star Alliance network.
  • Upgrades: Use your miles to upgrade your seat from Economy to Business Class or to the luxurious Polaris Business Class.
  • Hotel Stays and Car Rentals: Miles can also be redeemed for hotel stays and car rentals, offering even more flexibility in how you use your rewards.
  • Shopping and Experiences: MileagePlus members can redeem miles for shopping, dining, and unique experiences through United’s rewards partners.

Benefits of Achieving Elite Status

For frequent flyers, achieving elite status within the United MileagePlus Program can unlock a range of exclusive perks. The more you fly, the closer you get to enjoying these benefits:

  • Priority Boarding: Enjoy faster boarding with priority status.
  • Complimentary Upgrades: Eligible members may receive free upgrades to premium cabins, including Economy Plus and Business Class.
  • Extra Baggage Allowance: Members with elite status can take advantage of additional checked baggage allowances at no extra cost.
  • Lounge Access: Higher-tier members gain access to United Club Lounges and Star Alliance Lounges around the world.

How to Join the United MileagePlus Program

Joining the MileagePlus Program is free and easy. Simply visit the United Airlines website, create an account, and start earning miles right away. Whether you’re booking flights or shopping with United’s partners, every transaction brings you closer to rewards and benefits.
