How to Earn and Redeem KrisFlyer Miles for Free Flights with Singapore Airlines


One of the many perks of flying with Singapore Airlines is their KrisFlyer loyalty program, which rewards frequent travelers with miles that can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, and other travel-related benefits. Whether you fly often for business or leisure, understanding how to maximize your KrisFlyer miles can lead to incredible savings and exclusive perks, including free flights.


In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to earn KrisFlyer miles and how you can use them to unlock free flights with Singapore Airlines and its partner airlines.


1. Earning KrisFlyer Miles

Earning KrisFlyer miles is easier than you think. Below are several ways you can accumulate miles:

  • Flying with Singapore Airlines or SilkAir: Every time you fly with Singapore Airlines, SilkAir, or any of their partner airlines, you earn KrisFlyer miles. The number of miles you earn depends on your route, fare class, and the distance traveled.
  • Partner Airlines and Star Alliance: Singapore Airlines is part of the Star Alliance, so you can also earn KrisFlyer miles when flying with other member airlines such as United Airlines, Lufthansa, and Air New Zealand. These miles accumulate into your KrisFlyer account, giving you more opportunities to redeem them later.
  • Hotel Stays and Car Rentals: KrisFlyer has partnerships with several hotels and car rental companies. Booking your stays and rentals with these partners will allow you to earn additional miles for every dollar you spend.
  • Credit Card Spend: Many banks offer co-branded KrisFlyer credit cards that allow you to earn miles on everyday purchases. From groceries to online shopping, every swipe gets you closer to your next free flight.
  • Online Shopping: Through the KrisFlyer Spree online shopping portal, you can earn miles by making purchases from popular online stores. It’s a great way to boost your miles balance without even leaving your home.

2. How to Redeem KrisFlyer Miles for Free Flights

Once you’ve accumulated enough KrisFlyer miles, redeeming them for free flights is simple and rewarding. Here’s how you can do it:

  • Redeem Miles for Singapore Airlines Flights: Log in to your KrisFlyer account and browse flight availability. You can redeem miles for Economy, Premium Economy, Business, and First Class tickets on Singapore Airlines and its subsidiaries. Depending on your miles balance, you can cover the full cost of a ticket or reduce the price through part miles, part cash options.
  • Upgrade Your Seat: If you already have a ticket, you can use your KrisFlyer miles to upgrade your seat to a higher class, such as upgrading from Economy to Business Class. This is especially useful on long-haul flights where comfort matters most.
  • Redeem Miles on Partner Airlines: KrisFlyer miles can also be redeemed for flights on partner airlines within the Star Alliance network, offering flexibility and additional route options.
  • Pay for Fees and Charges: Use your KrisFlyer miles to pay for extra services such as excess baggage, seat selection, and airport lounge access—enhancing your travel experience without spending extra cash.

3. Maximizing KrisFlyer Miles

To get the most out of your KrisFlyer miles, consider these strategies:

  • Book Early: Award availability is often better when you book early. Premium cabin seats, like Business and First Class, can fill up quickly, so planning ahead is key.
  • Take Advantage of Promotions: Singapore Airlines regularly offers KrisFlyer Spontaneous Escapes, discounted mileage redemptions for selected flights. Keep an eye out for these promotions to use fewer miles on flights.
  • Combine with Other Rewards: If you have other frequent flyer points, you might be able to transfer them to your KrisFlyer account, boosting your balance and accelerating your journey toward a free flight.
