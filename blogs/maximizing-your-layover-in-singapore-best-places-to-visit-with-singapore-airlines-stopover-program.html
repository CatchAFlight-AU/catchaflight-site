Maximizing Your Layover in Singapore: Best Places to Visit with Singapore Airlines Stopover Program


If you’re flying with Singapore Airlines and have a layover in Singapore, you’re in for a treat. Singapore Airlines offers its travelers the Singapore Stopover Program, which allows you to explore the vibrant and multicultural city of Singapore while waiting for your connecting flight. Whether you have just a few hours or an overnight stay, there are plenty of exciting attractions and experiences to enjoy.


Here’s how to make the most of your layover in Singapore with some of the best places to visit through Singapore Airlines’ Stopover Program.


1. Marina Bay Sands

An iconic symbol of Singapore’s skyline, Marina Bay Sands is a must-visit during your layover. The hotel’s SkyPark Observation Deck offers spectacular panoramic views of the city, including Gardens by the Bay and the Singapore Flyer. If you have more time, enjoy the infinity pool or visit the luxury Shoppes at Marina Bay Sands for high-end shopping and dining.


2. Gardens by the Bay

Just a short walk from Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay is one of Singapore’s top attractions. The park is home to the stunning Supertree Grove, with towering tree-like structures that light up in a dazzling display at night. Don’t miss the Cloud Forest, with its indoor waterfall and exotic plant life, or the Flower Dome, where you can see flowers and plants from around the world.


3. Sentosa Island

For a more leisurely layover, head to Sentosa Island, known for its beaches and entertainment options. Visit the S.E.A. Aquarium, one of the largest aquariums in the world, or experience the thrills at Universal Studios Singapore. If you’re looking to relax, take a stroll along Siloso Beach or visit one of the island’s luxurious beach clubs.


4. Chinatown

For a cultural immersion, explore the vibrant streets of Chinatown. Visit the Buddha Tooth Relic Temple, stroll through the markets, and enjoy authentic Chinese cuisine at the local hawker stalls. Chinatown offers a fascinating look into Singapore’s Chinese heritage, making it a perfect stop if you’re short on time.


5. Little India

If you’re looking to experience Singapore’s rich diversity, visit Little India. This bustling district is known for its vibrant colors, intricate temples, and mouth-watering Indian cuisine. Take a walk down Serangoon Road, visit the Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple, and indulge in traditional Indian sweets and dishes at the local eateries.


6. Orchard Road

For the ultimate shopping experience, head to Orchard Road, Singapore’s premier shopping district. With a range of luxury boutiques, department stores, and malls, Orchard Road is a haven for shopaholics. If you have a little more time, enjoy a relaxing meal at one of the upscale restaurants or a cup of coffee at a trendy cafe.


7. Singapore Flyer

The Singapore Flyer offers one of the best views of the city. This giant Ferris wheel provides a bird’s-eye view of the iconic Marina Bay skyline, the Singapore River, and even parts of Indonesia on a clear day. If you’re on a shorter layover and want to take in as much of Singapore as possible, the Singapore Flyer is the perfect stop.


8. Clarke Quay

If you prefer a laid-back experience, head over to Clarke Quay for a relaxing evening by the Singapore River. This vibrant area is filled with restaurants, bars, and clubs, making it the perfect spot to enjoy a meal or drink with a view. You can also take a scenic river cruise to see the city’s famous landmarks lit up at night.


9. Kampong Glam

Explore Singapore’s Malay and Muslim heritage in the historic Kampong Glam district. Visit the stunning Sultan Mosque with its impressive golden dome and stroll through Haji Lane, a narrow street known for its colorful murals, quirky boutiques, and cozy cafes. Kampong Glam is perfect for a relaxed, cultural experience during your layover.


10. Changi Airport Experiences

If you’re short on time, don’t worry—Changi Airport itself is an attraction. Consistently ranked as one of the best airports in the world, Changi offers plenty of ways to relax and have fun. From the indoor Rain Vortex at Jewel to butterfly gardens, movie theaters, and luxurious lounges, you’ll find plenty to keep you entertained without ever leaving the airport.


Why Take Advantage of the Singapore Stopover Program?

With the Singapore Stopover Program, Singapore Airlines passengers can enjoy a hassle-free exploration of Singapore. The program offers discounted hotel stays, airport transfers, and admission to many of Singapore’s top attractions. Whether you’re on a short stopover or have a day to spare, the Stopover Program allows you to make the most of your layover and experience the best of Singapore.
