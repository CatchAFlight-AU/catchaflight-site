Frequently Asked Questions about Booking Cheap Flights

Q. How can I find the cheapest flight tickets?

Be flexible with your travel dates, book early, and consider mid-week departures. Our experts suggest flying on Tuesdays or Wednesdays for lower fares.

Q. Can I book my flight by phone?

Yes. Our travel experts specialize in phone reservations, making it fast and easy to book flights.

Q. How do I change or cancel a flight reservation?

Call our agents anytime. We’ll assist you with flight changes, cancellations, or refunds.

Q. Is it possible to upgrade my seat over the phone?

Absolutely. We can help you upgrade to Business, Premium, or First Class during your booking call.

Q. When is the best time to book international flights?

We recommend booking 6 to 10 weeks in advance for international trips. Prices typically rise the closer you get to your travel date.

Q. What time of day is best to book cheap flights online?

Flight prices can be lower during early mornings or late evenings, especially mid-week when demand is lower.

Q. Which airlines usually offer low-cost international tickets?

Budget airlines like Jetstar, Batik Air, Air Asia, Scoot often offer competitive international fares. We compare prices across 500+ airlines to find you the best deal.

Q. How early should I arrive at the airport?

For international departures, arrive at the airport 3 hours before your scheduled flight. For domestic flights, aim for 2 hours ahead of time.

Q. Can I cancel or change my flight after booking?

Yes, but change and cancellation policies vary by airline and ticket type. Our travel advisors can guide you through the process and options available.

Q. Is it true that flight prices vary based on browser or device?

Sometimes cookies can affect pricing shown. Use private browsing or call our team directly for the most accurate and up-to-date pricing.

Q. What if I need to book a last-minute flight?

No problem. Our Australia-based travel team can help you secure last-minute flight deals—just call us at (02) 7255 9388.

Q. Do you offer discounts for seniors, students, or military personnel?

Yes, we often have exclusive discounts for seniors, students, military members, and group travelers. Speak to our agents to check current offers.
