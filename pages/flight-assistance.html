Frequently Asked Questions about Booking Cheap Flights

Q. How can I find the cheapest flight tickets?

Be flexible with your travel dates, book early, and consider mid-week departures. Our experts suggest flying on Tuesdays or Wednesdays for lower fares.

Q. When is the best time to book international flights?

We recommend booking 6 to 10 weeks in advance for international trips. Prices typically rise the closer you get to your travel date.

Q. What time of day is best to book cheap flights online?

Flight prices can be lower during early mornings or late evenings, especially mid-week when demand is lower.

Q. Which airlines usually offer low-cost international tickets?

Budget airlines like JetBlue, Spirit, Norse Atlantic, PLAY, and AirAsia often offer competitive international fares. We compare prices across 500+ airlines to find you the best deal.

Q. How early should I arrive at the airport?

For international departures, arrive at the airport 3 hours before your scheduled flight. For domestic flights, aim for 2 hours ahead of time.
