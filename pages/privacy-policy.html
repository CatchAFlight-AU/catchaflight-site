Privacy Policy

At Catchaflight, your privacy and the security of your personal information are our top priorities...

What Information Do We Collect?

  • Personal Data: Full Name, Age, Gender, Date of Birth
  • Contact Data: Phone Number, Postal Address, Email Address
  • Booking Data: Flight and Travel Details, Passport Number, Travel Itinerary
  • Billing Information: Credit/Debit Card Details, Bank Account Details
  • Customer Contacts: With your permission, we may access your contacts to notify them about bookings or travel itineraries.
  • Customer Reviews & Content: We collect your reviews, ratings, and any travel-related content you share with us, helping us improve our services.
  • Social Media Data: Optional data when you link your social media accounts to our site, which may include photos, videos, and social content to help personalize your travel experience.

How Do We Collect Your Information?

We gather customer information from the following sources:

  • Direct Communication: When you interact with our customer service teams.
  • Customer Portals: Through bookings or account activities on our website.
  • Third-party Services: From trusted partners.
  • Surveys and Feedback Forms: To improve our services and customer experience.
  • Automated Technologies: Cookies and tracking tools that improve your website experience.
How Do We Protect Your Information?

We use state-of-the-art encryption technologies and follow industry standards to safeguard your personal data.
